Scan Barcodes and their price should appear on the screen.
To change the price of an item, type a number and then hit /
To change the quantity of an item, type a number and then hit *

To pay for the items double tap enter and you should see a payment screen.
Type the amount of cash the customer has given and then hit /
The screen should reset and show you the change back.

Type 000 and then hit enter to logout and close the POS
Type 000000 and then hit enter to come back to this page.
Type 000000000 and then hit enter to open the setting page. Settings page will only open if you are a manager.

Press Enter to Exit Help Page
